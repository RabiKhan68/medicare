Frequently Asked Questions

How long does therapy typically last?

+

Therapy duration varies based on your needs. Most clients see improvement within 8-16 sessions, though some continue longer for deeper work.

Is everything confidential?

+

Yes, all sessions are completely confidential with limited exceptions required by law. Your privacy and trust are paramount.

Do you accept insurance?

+

Yes, I accept most major insurance plans. Please contact me to verify your specific coverage.

Can I reschedule sessions?

+

I allow rescheduling with at least 24 hours notice to ensure I have availability for your new time slot.
